Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Fresh & Flavorful Chicken with Citrus and Herbs

This grilled lemon herb chicken is juicy, zesty, and packed with fresh herbs. It's the perfect healthy and flavorful meal for summer gatherings or quick weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, zest, garlic, parsley, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a large zip-top bag or shallow dish. Pour marinade over chicken, seal, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 8 hours for more flavor).
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade and discard remaining marinade.
  4. Grill chicken for 6–7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve with fresh herbs and lemon wedges if desired.
