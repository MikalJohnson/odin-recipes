Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Rich & Velvety Pasta Dish

This creamy garlic Parmesan pasta is a quick and comforting dish made with simple ingredients. Perfect for busy nights, this recipe is rich, cheesy, and guaranteed to be a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine or pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until cheese is melted and sauce is smooth.
  5. Toss cooked pasta into the sauce and mix until evenly coated. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
