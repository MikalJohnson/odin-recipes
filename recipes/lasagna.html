Classic Beef & Cheese Lasagna Recipe

This hearty and comforting lasagna is made with layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or meal prep, it’s a satisfying dish that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 (24oz) jar of marinara sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 15 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat with a bit of olive oil. Add chopped onion and cook until soft (2–3 minutes). Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat. Stir in marinara sauce, tomato paste (if using), oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low for 10–15 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, 1 cup of mozzarella, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). In a 9x13 inch baking dish: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer 3 noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture. Add a layer of meat sauce. Repeat steps for 2 more layers. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover with foil (spray foil with non-stick spray so it doesn't stick to the cheese). Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing.
